Imogen Alexandra

Experience Designer

Profile

Imogen is an Experience Designer at Condé Nast International, one of the world’s leading publishers. Housing brands such as Vogue, GQ, Wired and Vanity Fair, Condé Nast set the benchmark globally for delivering beautiful, influential products and brand experiences. Imogen leverages user insights to shape the design and strategy of Vogue’s products and services in 11 countries across Asia, Europe and Latin America. With a dual design / research background, she brings users to the forefront throughout the design process in order to solve the business challenges presented by the fast-changing and diverse global media landscape. Imogen holds a BSc in Psychology from University College London and an Art & Design Foundation Diploma from Central Saint Martins.
